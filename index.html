The conflict between Trump and Obamacare

Ever since Trump became the president of USA in 2017, he has been working towards repealing Obamacare and the Affordable Care Act once and for all. One of many reasons that he is putting so much efforts to repeal these acts is that the ACA has made it compulsory for most Americans to purchase their own insurance. As a result of the ACA, most Americans would have to limit their weekly incomes just to pay for Medicare premiums.

According to Trump's tweets between 2017 and and 2020, it is evident that he was very vocal about demolishing the act. However, not many medias or people supported his views and decisions, specially in 2020, where the pandemic began (Dorning, M., Dennis, T. S., (2020, June)).
